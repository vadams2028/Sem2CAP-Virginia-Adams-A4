Best Work

To showcase my best work, I chose my CAP project from semester 1. When designing this app, I faced challenges with the output as many people voted for Jim on my survey (and not many people voted for Pam, Kelly or Dwight), the output of my app was usually Jim. I found that collecting enough data (and accurate data) as well as getting different outputs was difficult when designing and coding my app. Despite these challenges, I think this is my best work because it showed all the skills I learned in semester 1. I enjoyed creating it because it has to do with my interests and I love taking buzzfeed quizzes so making my own quiz was very fun! I also enjoyed gathering data for my project and picking the images and aesthetic of of the quiz.
