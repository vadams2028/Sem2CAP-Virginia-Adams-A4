Growth

I think this project shows my growth in Computer Science because I was struggling throughout Unit 6 and seeing a good final product showed me how much I improved. For me, the most enjoyable part of this project was picking the image and designing my scene, I enjoyed seeing my ideas come to life with code; and the most challenging part of the project was drawing the smile and figuring out the correct arc. But I loved drawing the earth and stars. I saw my improvment specifically in my score, health and when I was using functions in coding. I got past these obstacles by looking back at past assignments and using them to help me understand what I was doing wrong.
